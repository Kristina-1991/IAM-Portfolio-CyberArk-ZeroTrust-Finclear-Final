## 3. Zero Trust Controls Mapping – How CyberArk Delivers Wave 1

| Zero Trust Pillar | Simple question | CyberArk Wave 1 Control | Result Dec 2025 |
|-------------------|------------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| **Identity**      | Who are you?     | • JIT + dual approval<br>• No standing privileges | 93 % JIT sessions<br>11 min approval |
| **Devices**       | Is your device safe? | • All sessions via PSM (no direct access)<br>• HTML5 gateway for cloud | 100 % sessions proxied |
| **Network**       | Is the network trusted? | • Zero direct RDP/SSH<br>• Micro-segmentation via PSM | No inbound connections to targets |
| **Applications** | What can you access? | • Safes + platforms segregation<br>• Automatic rotation (CPM)<br>• Owner assignment | 96.8 % accounts in CyberArk |
| **Data**          | What are you doing? | • 100 % session recording + playback<br>• Real-time Splunk export | 100 % sessions recorded & searchable |
