# 24-Month Privileged Access Management Roadmap
**Vision** → From reactive cleanup (2025) to fully automated, Zero-Trust privileged access by end-2027

| Quarter       | Milestone                                      | Key Deliverables                                                                 | Business Outcome                              |
|---------------|------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| **2025 Q4**   | **Foundational Controls Live**                 | • BR-00 → BR-03 fully deployed<br>• ≥ 95 % critical accounts in CyberArk<br>• JIT + dual approval + full recording operational | Eliminates €500k fraud risk<br>Closes NIS2/DORA gaps |
| **2026 Q1**   | **Automation & Integration**                   | • SailPoint ↔ CyberArk sync (termination events, metadata)<br>• Auto-onboarding of new cloud accounts (AWS/Azure)<br>• Just-In-Time for SaaS admins (M365, Salesforce) | Reduces onboarding from 4 h → < 30 min<br>Zero standing privileges |
| **2026 Q2**   | **SCADA / OT Full Rollout**                    | • Full SCADA environment (2 500+ devices)<br>• Air-gapped PSM for OT<br>• Custom connectors for legacy protocols | OT privileged access secured<br>DORA critical infrastructure compliance |
| **2026 Q3**   | **Advanced Threat Detection**                  | • UEBA on privileged sessions (CyberArk EPM + Splunk)<br>• Real-time session termination on anomaly<br>• Behavioural baselining per role | Early detection of Golden Ticket, lateral movement |
| **2026 Q4**   | **Zero Standing Privileges Enterprise-wide**   | • 100 % of privileged access via JIT (including break-glass)<br>• Permanent admin rights removed everywhere<br>• Full API-driven automation | True Zero Trust PAM achieved |
| **2027 Q1-Q2**| **Continuous Adaptive Access**                | • Risk-based session duration (short session if high risk)<br>• Integration with Microsoft Sentinel + CyberArk Identity Security Platform<br>• Self-service password vaulting for developers | Most mature PAM program in Belgian financial sector |
| **2027 Q3-Q4**| **Innovation & Optimisation**                  | • Passwordless privileged access (FIDO2 + certificates)<br>• Quantum-resistant encryption readiness<br>• Annual penetration test with red team | Future-proof against 2030+ threats |

**Outcome**  
From a 2024 situation with 150 unmanaged accounts and €750k in fines → to a **fully automated, adaptive, Zero-Trust PAM platform recognised as best-in-class in Belgium by 2027**.
